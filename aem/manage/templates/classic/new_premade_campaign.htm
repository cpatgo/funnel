7. {"Thank You / Redirect Page"|alang}

{"Thank You / Redirect Page"|alang}

What is a Thank you Page?

A "Thank you page" is the page your visitors see after submitting their information on a form.

What is a Redirect Page?

A Redirect Page is a particular page of your choice that you can send your Visitors after submitting form information.

Here you have 3 options. You can choose from one of our pre-made "Thank You" page templates, you can build your own "Thank You" page using the Site Builder, or add a custom URL link.

{"Create Campaign Download Page"|alang}

A "Download page" is the page your Visitors will be sent to after confirming their subscription from the system generated email.

From this page you can offer a Free E-book giveaway, you can have offer a free informational Video, or you can customize this page to upsell your offer.
